german corporation for international cooperation gmbh n 1 is a german development agency headquartered in bonn and eschborn that provides services in the field of international development cooperation giz mainly implements technical cooperation projects of the federal ministry for economic cooperation and development bmz its main commissioning party although it also works with the private sector and other national and supranational government organizations but usually not with non-governmental organizations on a public benefit basis in its activities giz seeks to follow the paradigm of
